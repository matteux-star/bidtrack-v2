# Summary

I work as a bid writer, one pain point i have identified is the lack of a simple, clean, and efficient tool for tracking a bid pipeline. Everyone uses spreadsheets, spreadsheets get messy, and we lose track of them. I want to build a simple, web based tool for SME's and similar sized businesses to eliminate this pain point. 


# Requirements

Lean, NOT a full CRM, highly secure, GDPR compliant, auth properly implemented, clients can download their own data with ease for regular backups of pipeline. 

## Requirements - Functionality
1. Homepage displaying essential information about pipeline
    1.1 Total opportunities, due within the next week, due this month, etc other similar essential info at a glance
2. Opportunities page where new bids can be logged
    2.1 we must decide on a good workflow and accompanying UI that allows all stages of the tender to be added at the ingestion of a new opportunity, along side value, opportunity name, region, etc
3. Detailed reporting page where users can view pipeline performance over the past 90/180/360 days etc, downloadable pdf reports would be nice to have but not essential
4. Scaffolding in place for multi seat structure, EG a business can have one bid manager with edit permissions, bid writer with view, sales team members with view, etc.
